FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What documents do I need to provide to my lawyer?

Provide all relevant documents related to your case, such as contracts, correspondence, and any legal notices received. Full disclosure helps your lawyer assess the situation accurately.

What Are the Payment Methods You Accept?

How do I choose the right lawyer for my case?

Look for a lawyer with expertise in the relevant area of law, check their experience, and consider client reviews. Make full use of dynamic filtering to find the right LSP.

How does the legal system in our country work, and what are the different courts?

India has a three-tiered system – lower courts, high courts, and the Supreme Court. Lower courts handle district-level cases, high courts oversee multiple states, and the Supreme Court is the highest appellate court.

Do I Really Need an Attorney?

Share details of your case and you can get help from the interns and LSPs. They will guide you about the steps that should be taken.

What are the potential costs involved in hiring a lawyer ?

Legal fees vary based on the complexity of the case, lawyer’s experience, and the location. Discuss fees upfront, including consultation charges and any additional expenses on real time chat.
